WHAT'S LINE CIVIC INSTITUTION STANDS FOR?

⭕ Civic Struggle

The goal of civil struggle is to disobey unjust and immoral laws in a non-violent manner. The goal is that these defiance's will destroy the possibility of their application and in the long run, just laws and legislators will rule the society. In fact, the civil struggle destroys the means of repression, intimidation and the development of injustice.

⭕ Breaking the law

Many people oppose civil war because of the element of law-breaking as its practical basis. They generally consider this to cause chaos. Some consider breaking the law as immoral. The answer is clear. Breaking the law in civil struggle does not cause chaos. It is these unjust laws that create chaos. Bad rules of life affect a part of a society or all its people, cause people to be killed and ruin their lives.

Vision and Mision

Vision

Line Civic Institution is a democratic and prosperous Afghanistan where every citizen is informed, engaged, and empowered. We strive to provide high-quality civic education, foster civic dialogue, and promote civic participation among the Afghan people. Our vision is to create a culture of active citizenship and social responsibility that contributes to the peace and development of our country.

Mision

Line Civic Institution is a non-profit Institution that aims to promote civic education, engagement, and empowerment in Afghanistan. We believe that every citizen has the right and responsibility to participate in the democratic process and contribute to the development of their society.

We provide various programs and services to help citizens learn about their rights and duties, connect with their communities and leaders, and take action on the issues that matter to them. Our mission is to foster a culture of active citizenship and social responsibility among the Afghan people.

Line Civic Institution Values

  • Human Rights
  • Social Justice
  • Diversity
  • Collaboration

Line Civic Institution’s Goals

  • Provide high-quality civic education
  • Foster civic dialogue
  • Promote civic participation among the Afghan Youths and adolescents

Line Civic Institution’s strategies
