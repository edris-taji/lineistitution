Committees

  • LINE
  • Committees

Fundraising and Development Committee

The Fundraising and Development Committee is responsible for securing financial resources to support the institution's initiatives. They strategize and execute fundraising campaigns, seek sponsorships, and cultivate relationships with donors to ensure the institution's sustainability and growth.

Governance and Ethics Committee

The Governance and Ethics Committee ensures transparency, accountability, and adherence to ethical standards within the civic institution. They develop and review policies, monitor compliance, and provide guidance to maintain integrity and uphold the organization's values.

Volunteer and Volunteerism Committee

This committee oversees the institution's volunteer program, recruiting and managing volunteers who contribute their time and expertise. They assess volunteer needs, provide training and support, and recognize volunteers' efforts in advancing the institution's mission.

Education Committee

This committee focuses on developing and improving educational programs and initiatives within the civic institution. They collaborate with educators, administrators, and community members to ensure quality education and promote lifelong learning.

Sustainability Committee

The Sustainability Committee works towards making the institution environmentally responsible and sustainable. They develop and implement strategies for reducing energy consumption, managing waste, promoting conservation, and raising awareness about sustainable practices.

Health and Wellness Committee

The Health and Wellness Committee promotes physical and mental well-being within the institution. They organize health-related events, provide resources on nutrition and fitness, and advocate for a healthy work environment.

Arts and Culture Committee

The Arts and Culture Committee aims to enrich the community by promoting artistic expression and cultural appreciation. They organize exhibitions, performances, workshops, and events that celebrate various forms of art, music, dance, and theater.

Outreach & Engagement Committee

The Outreach and Engagement Committee focuses on engaging with the local community and expanding the institution's reach. They establish partnerships, organize community outreach programs, and promote active participation to foster a sense of belonging and inclusion.
