Salar's Profile

Sharifullah Karamat Salar is a remarkable individual who has dedicated his life to advocating for human rights, fostering social change, and empowering marginalized communities. As the founder of Line Civil Institution, he has played a pivotal role in initiating and leading impactful initiatives that address pressing social issues.

With a Master's degree in Social Science, Sharifullah brings deep knowledge and expertise in understanding the complexities of societal challenges. His academic background, coupled with his unwavering commitment to human rights, allows him to develop comprehensive strategies for promoting sustainable and inclusive development.

Mr. Salar's possesses an exceptional ability to engage with diverse communities, listening to their concerns and mobilizing resources to address their specific needs. Through Line Civil Institution, he has spearheaded projects that focus on issues like education, sustainability, arts and culture, outreach, and volunteerism, making a tangible difference in the lives of individuals and Afghan communities.

Recognized as a passionate and dedicated human rights activist, Sharifullah has actively worked to advance the rights of marginalized groups, particularly advocating for gender equality and empowerment. He firmly believes that women's participation and representation are vital for achieving social progress and has championed initiatives to create safe spaces and promote women's political participation.

Beyond his involvement in the Line Civil Institution, Sharifullah has collaborated with national and international organizations to promote human rights, raise awareness, and advocate for policy reform. His leadership and strategic vision have earned him respect and admiration among his peers and communities he serves.

As a compassionate and forward-thinking leader, Sharifullah continuously seeks innovative approaches to address social injustices, inspire community engagement, and foster sustainable change. He firmly believes in the power of collective action and encourages collaboration between institutions, grassroots organizations, and government entities to create a more equitable and just society.

Mr. Salar's unwavering dedication to social justice, human rights, and community empowerment has made him a valuable leader and advocate. Through his visionary leadership and commitment to building a better world, he continues to inspire others to take action and work towards a more inclusive and equitable society.
