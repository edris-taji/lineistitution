Who Am I?

Sharifullah Karamat Salar was born in 1992 in in a science-loving and culture-loving family in Herat, Afghanistan.

In 2010, he successfully graduated from Amir Ali Shir Navaei High School in Herat and started the Faculty of Law and Political Sciences, Department of Diplomacy in Ghalib Private University.

After completing his bachelor's degree in 2016, he traveled to India to continue his studies and successfully completed his master's degree of political science in Pune International University, India.

Karamat Salar founded the ASAS (American System for Afghan Students) Academy and taught English for a long time.

In 2014 After successfully passing the online exam, he accepted to Regis University for an online degree in liberal studies by studying the first online semester he got the idea of establishing a social institution to struggle for Human Rights, Women Identity, Women Empowerment, Afghan youth capacity building, liberal studies meetings and seminars for afghan youths.

He was also a University Professors for a time and still he is keeping on his struggle
